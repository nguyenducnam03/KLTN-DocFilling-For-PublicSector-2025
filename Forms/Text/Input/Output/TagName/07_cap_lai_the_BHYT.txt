**Explanation of Tag Names:**

* **[receiver_full_name]**: Full name of the person or organization submitting the form.
* **[receiver_unit_name]**: Name of the unit (if applicable) representing the person submitting the form.
* **[receiver_unit_code]**: Code of the unit (if applicable) representing the person submitting the form.
* **[user1_full_name]**: Full name of the user applying for health insurance.
* **[user1_health_insurance_card_number]**: Health insurance card number of the user.
* **[user1_health_insurance_registration_place]**: Place where the user registered for health insurance.
* **[user1_current_address]**: Current address of the user.
* **[user1_phone]**: Phone number of the user.
* **[user1_email]**: Email address of the user.
* **[request_content]**: Content or specific request of the user in the form.
* **[decision_duration]**: Duration for processing the request.
* **[decision_day]**: Day of the decision related to the user's request.
* **[decision_month]**: Month of the decision related to the user's request.
* **[decision_year]**: Year of the decision related to the user's request.
* **[user1_bank_account]**: Bank account number of the user.
* **[user1_bank_name]**: Bank name of the user. 

This approach ensures consistency and accuracy by using a structured naming convention for all placeholders, making it easier to manage and analyze the information collected.
