**Newly defined tag names:**

*   **id_card_issued_day:** Day when the ID card/CCCD was issued.
*   **id_card_issued_month:** Month when the ID card/CCCD was issued.
*   **id_card_issued_year:** Year when the ID card/CCCD was issued.
*   **passport\_number:** Passport number.
*   **passport\_issued\_day:** Day when the passport was issued.
*   **passport\_issued\_month:** Month when the passport was issued.
*   **passport\_issued\_year:** Year when the passport was issued.
*   **passport\_expiry\_day:** Day when the passport expires.
*   **passport\_expiry\_month:** Month when the passport expires.
*   **passport\_expiry\_year:** Year when the passport expires.
*   **passport\_issued\_place:** The authority that issued the passport.
*   **visa\_number:** Visa number.
*   **visa\_expiry\_date:** Visa expiry date.
*   **visa\_issued\_country:** Country that issued the visa.
